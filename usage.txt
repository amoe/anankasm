Usage: naturalize [OPTION]... MP3...
Integrate MP3s into a tagged collection.

  -M, --mastering-disparity    do not scan album gain
  -P, --mangle-mtimes          do not preserve file mtimes
  -v, --verbose                show output from subprocesses
  -V, --va-mode                various artists mode

naturalize will tag, add ReplayGain information, and rename files
appropriately.

Report bugs to <amoebae@gmail.com>.
